Hi, I’m Dr. Bittu Mandal

Economist & Population Researcher

I work at the intersection of internal migration, ageing, and health inequalities in India. Using large-scale surveys such as LASI and NFHS, my research examines how migration trajectories, urban exposure, and functional limitations shape later-life health and wellbeing.

About Me

Migration, Ageing & Health

I am a Fellow at the National Centre for Good Governance (NCGG), Government of India, based in New Delhi. My work focuses on evaluation and strengthening of public service delivery systems, including Right to Service (RTS) Acts and digital governance portals across Indian states.

I completed my Ph.D. in Economics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Indore, under the supervision of Dr. Kalandi Charan Pradhan. I also hold degrees in Population Studies (IIPS Mumbai) and Geography (BHU, University of Burdwan). My broader interest lies in understanding how mobility, place, and institutions shape wellbeing over the life course.

Research

Migrants’ mental health

Examining depression among later-life migrants, with a focus on age at migration, functional limitations, and cumulative disadvantage.

Urban exposure & obesity

Studying how timing, duration, and intensity of urban residence influence obesity and cardiometabolic risk among middle- and older-aged migrants.

Hypertension & chronic disease

Assessing links between rural-to-urban migration, hypertension onset, and multimorbidity in later life using longitudinal and cross-sectional data.

Migration, ageing & disparities

Comparing health outcomes between migrant and non-migrant older adults in India using matching, decomposition, and life-course approaches.

Policy Work

At NCGG, I contribute to evaluation and advisory work for Union and State governments in India. Recent engagements include:

  • Assessment of Right to Service (RTS) Acts in Haryana, Maharashtra, and Madhya Pradesh.
  • Analysis of digital governance portals such as SARAL, Aaple Sarkar, and MP e-District.
  • Support to impact assessments of national skill-development and public service delivery initiatives.
