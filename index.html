About

I am a Fellow (Consultant Grade I) at the National Centre for Good Governance (NCGG), Government of India. My work focuses on strengthening governance systems, public service delivery, digital platforms, and monitoring & evaluation frameworks across Indian states.

I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Indore. My research examines internal migration, ageing, health disparities, and the role of life-course transitions in shaping wellbeing in older adulthood. I work extensively with large-scale datasets such as LASI, NFHS, and administrative data systems.

Research

Internal Migration & Ageing

Later-life health trajectories, depression, multimorbidity, and the role of migration histories in shaping successful ageing.

Urban Exposure & Obesity

Impact of timing, duration, and intensity of urban residence on obesity and cardiometabolic risks among older migrants.

Mental Health & Functional Limitations

Functional disability, social exclusion, and pathways to depression among older adults in India.

Public Service Delivery

Administrative data analysis of Right to Service Acts, digital platforms, and governance reforms across Indian states.
